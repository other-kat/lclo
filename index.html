.

developers

.

_______________________

visuals

you want to create and design reactive visuals performances will be set to. nobody wants to just be staring at a group on stage looking into screens

.

_______________________

physicals

you want to make physical sounds with your laptop, this can be using a microphone or simply the sound of you tapping

.

_______________________

controllers

you want to play using software created for the performances. this involves learning and spending time with someone elses software.
